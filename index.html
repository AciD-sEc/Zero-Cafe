About Us

Dine-In


Zero-Cafe is a popular dessert destination for college students, local residents, and travellers. We provide friendly service and offer a scrumptious array of quality desserts in the busy college Shops Plaza located around karur.


Takeout or Delivery


We carry several takeout box sizes to fit all our menu items, so come on in and grab a delectable dessert to enjoy on the go.


We also provide delivery services.


Menu

Cake Slices

Matcha Swiss Roll ₹70/slice

Soft and fluffy sponge cake infused with matcha, perfectly balanced with creamy whipped cream filling. This guilty-free dessert has no butter or oil added, and it is packed with antioxidants.

Strawberry Cake Roll ₹50/slice

Light and airy sponge cake filled with fresh strawberries and delicate whipped cream. The cake is kept chilled until it is served, to bring out its refreshing qualities.

Chocolate Cream Roll ₹40/slice

Rich and irresistible chocolate sponge cake with whipped cream filling. The cake is made with real cocoa, without added sugar, and the whipped cream is also sugar-free.

Red Velvet Cake Roll ₹60/slice

Tender and moist cake stuffed with delicious cream cheese frosting that is as light and fluffy as whipped cream.
